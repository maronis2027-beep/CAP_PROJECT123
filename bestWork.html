Best Work

Personality Test

The project which was my “best work” (at least in my opinion) was our semester one CAP, the personality test. For my personality test, I did “What Friends Character are you based on your favorite Season.” I believe I was able to do my best work on this project because I had a sense of creative control unlike any other project we have worked on this school year. There were some bumps along the road, but in my opinion, this project was easy to execute in terms of coding (compared to other projects we have worked on.) However, this work stands out to me because I was able to dictate the design theme of the test itself, and I was able to use my creative freedom to incorporate a show that I loved, along with a design that I resonated with, while using images that I hand picked to create an overall aesthetic feel for the test. To make this work, I used my creative and artistic skills, the knowledge I had of what my peers wanted to see/what they liked, and the knowledge I had of the show “Friends” which was the premise of my project.
