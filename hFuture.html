Future

In this class, I have learned a lot about computer science. I have learned how to do many basic things, and many complicated things that I never thought I would get around to doing. To be specific, I have learned to analyze different situations, and to check my work as I go. Something that was hard for me in this class was making sure that my code was perfect. There were many times where I did not know what was wrong with my code, and it turned out to be one little thing. Because I have a more creative mind, I am used to thinking less perfectly, and more outside of the box; so, that was a struggle for me that I could continue to work on.
